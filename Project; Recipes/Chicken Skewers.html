Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:


  • 2 pouonds boneless skinless chicken
  • 4 bell peppers chopped into 1” pieces
  • 1 Tablespoon peanut oil
  • Garlic salt & pepper to taste

Peanut Sauce Marinade

  • 2 Tablespoons Peanut Butter (Creamy or Crunchy)
  • 2 TBS Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons Peanut Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon Sesame Oil
  • 2 Tablespoons Brown Sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon Rice Vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon Powdered Ginger
  • 1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • ¼ teaspoon Onion Powder

Instructions:

  1. At least one hour before cooking (preferably longer): Soak 12 bamboo skewers in warm water for 1-4 hours.
  2. Slice chicken breasts into strips about 2” long and ½ “ thick.
  3. Place chicken pieces into a glass dish that has a lid.

Make the Peanut Sauce:

  1. In a small bowl combine all the sauce ingredients and mix together until smooth. If using thick, solid peanut butter microwave ingredients for 30 seconds before mixing.

Marinate the Chicken

  1. Pour ¾ of the peanut sauce over the chicken in the bowl and stir until it’s evenly distributed, reserve the rest of the sauce to brush on the chicken while it is grilling.
  2. Secure the lid and let marinade for 30 minutes at room temperature, or longer in the refrigerator.

Prepare the Vegetables

  1. Chop peppers into 1” square pieces
  2. Place chopped peppers into a microwave safe dish and heat on high in the microwave for 4 minutes, or until they are soft to the touch but not fully cooked.
  3. Drizzle peanut oil over peppers and stir to combine.
  4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.

Assemble the Skewers

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Alternate threading the chicken and peppers onto the skewers until you have used all of the ingredients. Be sure to try to keep the chicken as flat as possible so it cooks evenly.

Grill the Skewers

  1. Place the kabobs on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side, turning them halfway through cooking.
  2. Use a meat thermometer to check for doneness (chicken should reach a temperature of 150 – 160 degrees Fahrenheit).
  3. Remove from the grill and serve immediately over rice or a salad!
